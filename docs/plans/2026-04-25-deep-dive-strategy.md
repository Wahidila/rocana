## Part A: Package Visual Branding

### A.1 Package Naming Philosophy

Setiap nama paket terinspirasi dari elemen Bali dan perjalanan spiritual — bukan angka generik ("Package 1, 2, 3"). Ini menciptakan emotional connection dan membuat klien merasa membeli *pengalaman*, bukan *jasa*.

| Tier | Name | Inspiration | Emotional Hook |
|------|------|-------------|----------------|
| $59 | **Bali Moment** | Satu momen singkat yang abadi | "Even 30 minutes in Bali can last forever" |
| $149 | **Golden Hour** | Waktu emas saat matahari terbenam di Bali | "The hour when Bali glows — and so will you" |
| $249 | **Island Story** | Setiap pulau punya cerita, begitu juga kamu | "Your story, told through the island's eyes" |
| $399 | **Soul Cinema** | Sinema jiwa — foto + video + udara | "Not just photos. A film about your soul." |
| $799 | **Eternal** | Abadi — kenangan yang tidak pernah pudar | "Some moments deserve to live forever" |
| Wedding | **Sacred** / **Devoted** / **Infinite** | Upacara suci, kesetiaan, tak terbatas | "Where two souls become one story" |

## Part B: Partnership Strategy

### B.1 Why Partnerships Matter

Saat ini klien datang dari Instagram dan word-of-mouth. Ini bagus tapi tidak scalable. Partnership membuka **3 channel baru sekaligus:**

1. **Hotels/Villas** = akses ke turis yang SUDAH di Bali dan punya budget
2. **Wedding Planners** = akses ke klien high-value yang PASTI butuh fotografer
3. **Tour Guides/Drivers** = "street team" yang ketemu turis setiap hari

### B.2 Partnership Tiers

#### Tier 1: Hotels & Villas (Highest Priority)

**Target:** Boutique hotels dan private villas di Ubud, Seminyak, Canggu, Uluwatu — yang sering dipakai turis asing untuk honeymoon, anniversary, atau family vacation.

**Value proposition untuk hotel:**
- Tambahan revenue tanpa effort (komisi per referral)
- Meningkatkan guest experience ("We can arrange a professional photographer for you")
- Konten gratis — Rocana bisa foto properti mereka sebagai barter

**Commission Structure:**

| Model | Detail | Best For |
|-------|--------|----------|
| **Flat referral fee** | $15-25 per booking yang berhasil | Villa managers, front desk staff |
| **Percentage commission** | 10-15% dari total booking | Hotel management, concierge desk |
| **Barter** | Free property photoshoot (10 foto) per 5 referrals | New/small properties yang butuh konten |

**Approach Strategy (Cold Outreach):**

```
Phase 1: Research (Week 1-2)
- Identify 30 boutique hotels/villas in target areas
- Check their Instagram — do they already recommend photographers?
- Find the concierge/manager contact (Instagram DM, email, visit)

Phase 2: First Contact (Week 3-4)
- Visit in person with a printed portfolio booklet + business card
- Bring a small gift (framed sample photo of their area)
- Pitch: "We'd love to be your recommended photographer for guests"

Phase 3: Formalize (Week 5-6)
- Send partnership proposal via email (see template below)
- Set up tracking system (unique referral code per property)
- Provide marketing materials (QR code card for guest rooms)
```

#### Tier 2: Wedding Planners & Event Organizers

**Target:** Wedding planners yang handle destination weddings di Bali untuk klien internasional.

**Value proposition:**
- Reliable, full-service vendor (foto + video + drone = satu vendor, bukan tiga)
- Consistent quality dan style
- Easy to work with — AI planning tools reduce coordination overhead
- Commission on every wedding referral

**Commission Structure:**

| Wedding Package | Planner Commission | Amount |
|----------------|-------------------|---------|
| Intimate ($1,200) | 10% | $120 |
| Classic ($2,200) | 12% | $264 |
| Grand ($3,500) | 15% | $525 |

**Approach Strategy:**

```
Phase 1: Identify (Week 1-2)
- Research top 20 Bali wedding planners on Google, Instagram, The Knot
- Check who they currently use as photographers
- Look for planners who DON'T have an exclusive photographer yet

Phase 2: Portfolio Delivery (Week 3-4)
- Create a dedicated "Wedding Planner Kit":
  • PDF lookbook (20 pages, best wedding work)
  • Pricing sheet (wholesale/partner rates)
  • Testimonials from past wedding clients
  • Link to full online portfolio
- Send via email + Instagram DM

Phase 3: Relationship Building (Ongoing)
- Offer to shoot one styled shoot for FREE with the planner
  (they get content, you get portfolio + relationship)
- Attend wedding industry events in Bali
- Join Bali Wedding Association if one exists
```

#### Tier 3: Tour Guides & Drivers (Street Team)

**Target:** Private drivers and tour guides who serve international tourists daily.

**Value proposition:**
- Easy passive income — just hand out a card or mention Rocana
- $10-15 flat fee per successful booking (cash or transfer)
- No effort required beyond a recommendation

**How it works:**

```
1. Recruit 10-15 reliable drivers/guides
2. Give each a stack of Rocana referral cards with unique code
3. When their tourist books through the card's QR code, 
   driver gets $10-15 per booking (paid weekly via transfer)
4. Top referrers get bonus: $50/month for 5+ referrals
```

**Why this works:** Drivers spend 8-10 hours/day with tourists. They're asked "do you know a good photographer?" constantly. Currently they recommend random freelancers. Give them a reason to recommend Rocana.

#### Tier 4: MUA / Hair Stylists (Mutual Referral)

**Target:** Freelance makeup artists who serve international clients in Bali.

**Model:** Pure mutual referral — no commission needed.
- Rocana recommends them to clients who need MUA (+$120 add-on, MUA keeps 100%)
- They recommend Rocana to their clients who need photography
- Collaborate on styled shoots for mutual portfolio building

## Part D: Client Experience Map

### D.1 The Full Journey — 8 Stages

```
DISCOVER → EXPLORE → PLAN → BOOK → PREPARE → SHOOT → DELIVER → ADVOCATE
```

### D.2 Stage-by-Stage Breakdown

---

#### Stage 1: DISCOVER
*"How do they find us?"*

| Touchpoint | Channel | Experience | Rocana Action |
|------------|---------|------------|---------------|
| Google search "bali photographer" | SEO | Finds Rocana on page 1, clicks through | Invest in SEO content from day 1 |
| Instagram explore/hashtag | Social | Sees stunning Rocana photo, taps profile | Post 3-4x/week with location tags |
| Hotel room QR card | Partnership | Sees card on nightstand, scans QR code | Distribute cards to partner hotels |

**Goal:** 500 visits/month by Month 3, 2,000 by Month 6

| **Blog** | SEO + expertise | Location guides, "What to Wear", Bali tips |
| **Contact/Book** | Convert | AI assistant chat, booking calendar, WhatsApp button |

**Critical UX rules:**
- Mobile-first (80%+ of tourists browse on phone)
- Page load under 3 seconds
- WhatsApp button floating on every page
- AI chat widget bottom-right on every page
- "From $59" visible above the fold on homepage

**Key metric:** Booking page visit rate (% of homepage visitors who reach pricing page)
**Goal:** 30%+ visit pricing page

#### Stage 3: PLAN
*"They're interested. Now they need help deciding."*

| Interaction | Channel | Experience |
|-------------|---------|------------|
| AI assistant conversation | Website chat | "Hi! I'm planning a trip to Bali with my partner. We want photos at rice terraces." → AI recommends Golden Hour or Island Story, suggests Tegalalang at 4:30pm |
| WhatsApp inquiry | WhatsApp | Quick personal response within 1 hour (during Bali business hours) |
| Email inquiry | Email | Detailed response with portfolio samples relevant to their request |
| AI Shoot Planner | Website tool | Client inputs date + preferences → receives custom shoot plan with locations, timing, outfit tips |

**Automation opportunities:**
- AI chatbot handles 70% of initial inquiries without human intervention
- Auto-send portfolio samples based on inquiry type (wedding → wedding portfolio)
- AI Shoot Planner generates PDF automatically

**Key metric:** Inquiry-to-booking conversion rate
**Goal:** 40%+ of inquiries convert to bookings

---

#### Stage 4: BOOK
*"They've decided. Make it frictionless."*

| Step | Action | Tool |
|------|--------|------|
| 1 | Select package on website | Package page with "Book Now" buttons |
| 2 | Choose date + time | Integrated calendar (Calendly or custom) |
| 3 | Select add-ons | Checkbox list with prices |
| 4 | Fill brief questionnaire | "Tell us about your shoot" — occasion, group size, style preference, locations interested |
| 5 | Payment | 50% deposit via Stripe/PayPal, remaining 50% on shoot day |
| 6 | Confirmation | Instant email + WhatsApp confirmation with booking details |
| 7 | AI Shoot Plan delivery | Within 24 hours — personalized PDF with locations, timing, outfit guide, weather forecast |

**Payment options:**
- Credit card (Stripe) — for international clients
- PayPal — alternative for those who prefer it
- Bank transfer — for local/Indonesian clients
- 50/50 split: 50% deposit to confirm, 50% on shoot day (or day before)

**Cancellation policy:**
- 7+ days before: full refund minus $20 processing fee
- 3-7 days before: 50% refund
- Under 3 days: no refund (reschedule available)
- Bad weather: free reschedule, no penalty

**Key metric:** Cart abandonment rate
**Goal:** Under 30% abandonment

#### Stage 5: PREPARE
*"Between booking and shoot day."*

| Timeline | Action | Channel |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Immediately after booking | Send confirmation + AI Shoot Plan PDF | Email + WhatsApp |
| 3 days before shoot | "Getting excited!" reminder with weather update, outfit reminder, meeting point | WhatsApp |
| 1 day before shoot | Final confirmation: time, location, photographer name, what to bring | WhatsApp |
| Morning of shoot | "See you at [time]! Weather looks [great/we have a backup plan]" | WhatsApp |

**AI Shoot Plan PDF includes:**
- Map with pin locations and route
- Golden hour time for the specific date
- Weather forecast
- Outfit color suggestions (based on location palette)
- "What to bring" checklist
- Photographer name + photo
- Emergency contact number

**Key metric:** No-show / late cancellation rate
**Goal:** Under 5%

---

#### Stage 6: SHOOT
*"The actual experience. This is where the magic happens."*

| Moment | Action | Impact |
|--------|--------|--------|
| **Arrival** | Photographer arrives 10 min early, greets warmly, explains the plan | First impression = professional |
| **Icebreaker** | 5 min casual chat, no camera yet. Learn names, story, what they're celebrating | Builds comfort, reduces stiffness |
| **Direction** | Gentle posing guidance: "Walk toward each other... now look at the view..." | Clients feel guided, not bossed |
| **Candid moments** | Capture in-between moments — laughing, adjusting hair, genuine reactions | These become the favorite photos |
| **Drone** | "Let's get an aerial shot!" — wow moment, clients feel special | Differentiator, creates excitement |
| **Video** | Videographer captures cinematic B-roll throughout, minimal direction needed | Seamless, doesn't interrupt photo flow |
| **Sneak peek** | At the end: "Let me show you one on the back of the camera" | Instant gratification, builds excitement for gallery |
| **Wrap-up** | Thank them, explain delivery timeline, ask if they'd like any add-ons | Upsell opportunity (extra Reel, rush delivery) |

**On-shoot upsell opportunities:**
- "Would you like a quick Reel edit for Instagram? We can add that for $50"
- "The light is incredible — want to extend 30 minutes for $50?"
- "We have a drone — want to add aerial shots for $75?"

**Key metric:** Client satisfaction (post-shoot survey score)
**Goal:** 4.8+ out of 5.0

---

#### Stage 7: DELIVER
*"The product. This is what they paid for."*

| Timeline | Deliverable | Channel |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| **Within 24 hours** | 5-20 sneak peek photos (depending on package) | WhatsApp + email with gallery link |
| **5-10 business days** | Full edited photo gallery | Private online gallery (Pic-Time, Pixieset, or CloudSpot) |
| **14-21 business days** | Cinematic video + Reel edits (if applicable) | Gallery + direct download link |
| **Same day (if add-on)** | 1 same-day edited photo | WhatsApp |

**Gallery experience:**
- Private link, password-protected
- Mobile-optimized (clients will view on phone)
- Download in full resolution
- Option to order prints/albums directly from gallery
- Gallery stays live for 6 months

**Sneak peek strategy (critical for marketing):**
- Send sneak peeks while client is STILL IN BALI
- They post on Instagram → tag @rocana.studio → free marketing
- Include a subtle watermark or "Shot by Rocana.Studio" in metadata

**Key metric:** Gallery delivery on time rate
**Goal:** 95%+ on time

#### Stage 8: ADVOCATE
*"Turn happy clients into marketing machines."*

| Timeline | Action | Channel |
|----------|--------|---------|
| **With gallery delivery** | "We'd love your feedback! Here's a quick link to leave a Google Review" | Email + WhatsApp |
| **1 day after delivery** | "Feel free to tag us @rocana.studio when you share your photos!" | WhatsApp |
| **1 week after** | "Thank you again! If any friends are visiting Bali, we'd love to photograph them too. Here's a $20 referral credit for you and your friend." | Email |
| **3 months after** | "Happy 3-month anniversary of your Bali trip! Here's a favorite memory from your gallery." | Email (automated) |
| **1 year after** | "One year ago today, we captured your story in Bali. Planning a return?" | Email (automated) |

**Review collection strategy:**
- Ask EVERY client for a Google Review (this is the #1 priority)
- Make it easy: send direct link to Google Review form
- Also ask for Flytographer/TripAdvisor review if applicable
- Offer incentive: "Leave a review and get 1 extra edited photo from your gallery"

**Referral program:**
- Client refers a friend → both get $20 off their next booking
- Track via unique referral code per client
- Announce on social media: "Refer a friend, both save $20"

**User-generated content:**
- Repost client photos/stories that tag @rocana.studio (with permission)
- Create "Client Stories" highlight on Instagram
- Feature client testimonials on website with their photos

**Key metrics:**
- Google Review rate: Goal 50%+ of clients leave a review
- Referral rate: Goal 10%+ of clients refer someone
- Social media tag rate: Goal 30%+ tag @rocana.studio
