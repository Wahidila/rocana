Part B — Partnership Strategy

Building the
Referral Network

Currently clients come from Instagram and word-of-mouth. That’s great but not scalable. Partnerships open 3 new channels simultaneously.

B.1 Why Partnerships Matter
Channel 1
Hotels & Villas

Access to tourists who are ALREADY in Bali and have budget. They’re staying at premium properties and looking for experiences.

Channel 2
Wedding Planners

Access to high-value clients who DEFINITELY need a photographer. Wedding planners are the gatekeepers to the highest-revenue bookings.

Channel 3
Tour Guides & Drivers

A “street team” that meets tourists every single day. They’re constantly asked “do you know a good photographer?” — give them a reason to say Rocana.

B.2 Partnership Tiers
Tier 1: Hotels & VillasHighest Priority

Target: Boutique hotels and private villas in Ubud, Seminyak, Canggu, Uluwatu — frequently used by international tourists for honeymoon, anniversary, or family vacation.

Value proposition for hotels:

  • Additional revenue without effort (commission per referral)
  • Enhanced guest experience (“We can arrange a professional photographer for you”)
  • Free content — Rocana can photograph their property as barter
Commission Structure
ModelDetailBest For
Flat referral fee$15–25 per successful bookingVilla managers, front desk staff
Percentage commission10–15% of total bookingHotel management, concierge desk
BarterFree property photoshoot (10 photos) per 5 referralsNew/small properties that need content
Approach Strategy (Cold Outreach)
Phase 1 — Week 1–2
Research
  • Identify 30 boutique hotels/villas in target areas
  • Check their Instagram — do they already recommend photographers?
  • Find the concierge/manager contact (Instagram DM, email, visit)
Phase 2 — Week 3–4
First Contact
  • Visit in person with a printed portfolio booklet + business card
  • Bring a small gift (framed sample photo of their area)
  • Pitch: “We’d love to be your recommended photographer for guests”
Phase 3 — Week 5–6
Formalize
  • Send partnership proposal via email (see template below)
  • Set up tracking system (unique referral code per property)
  • Provide marketing materials (QR code card for guest rooms)

Tier 2: Wedding Planners & Event OrganizersHigh Priority

Target: Wedding planners who handle destination weddings in Bali for international clients.

Value proposition:

  • Reliable, full-service vendor (photo + video + drone = one vendor, not three)
  • Consistent quality and style
  • Easy to work with — AI planning tools reduce coordination overhead
  • Commission on every wedding referral

Approach Strategy
Phase 1 — Week 1–2
Identify
  • Research top 20 Bali wedding planners on Google, Instagram, The Knot
  • Check who they currently use as photographers
  • Look for planners who DON’T have an exclusive photographer yet
Phase 2 — Week 3–4
Portfolio Delivery
  • Create a dedicated “Wedding Planner Kit”: PDF lookbook (20 pages), pricing sheet, testimonials, link to full online portfolio
  • Send via email + Instagram DM
Phase 3 — Ongoing
Relationship Building
  • Offer to shoot one styled shoot for FREE with the planner (they get content, you get portfolio + relationship)
  • Attend wedding industry events in Bali
  • Join Bali Wedding Association if one exists

Tier 3: Tour Guides & Drivers (Street Team)Medium Priority

Target: Private drivers and tour guides who serve international tourists daily.

Value proposition:

  • Easy passive income — just hand out a card or mention Rocana
  • $10–15 flat fee per successful booking (cash or transfer)
  • No effort required beyond a recommendation
How It Works
StepAction
1Recruit 10–15 reliable drivers/guides
2Give each a stack of Rocana referral cards with unique code
3When their tourist books through the card’s QR code, driver gets $10–15 per booking (paid weekly via transfer)
4Top referrers get bonus: $50/month for 5+ referrals
Why This Works

Drivers spend 8–10 hours/day with tourists. They’re asked “do you know a good photographer?” constantly. Currently they recommend random freelancers. Give them a reason to recommend Rocana.

Tier 4: MUA / Hair Stylists (Mutual Referral)Supportive

Target: Freelance makeup artists who serve international clients in Bali.

Model: Pure mutual referral — no commission needed.

  • Rocana recommends them to clients who need MUA (+$120 add-on, MUA keeps 100%)
  • They recommend Rocana to their clients who need photography
  • Collaborate on styled shoots for mutual portfolio building
