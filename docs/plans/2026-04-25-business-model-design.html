01 — Executive Summary

The Complete Bali
Creative Studio

Rocana.Studio is a Bali-based photography & videography studio targeting international tourists, expats, and destination event clients. The studio offers a full-service creative package — a combination that no single competitor in Bali currently provides under one brand.

Core Strategy: Enter the market with an accessible “hook” price ($59 for a basic session) to build volume, reviews, and SEO authority, then funnel clients into higher-value packages ($250–800+) through upselling. Differentiate through AI-powered booking/planning tools, full-service capability, and a distinctive earthy visual brand.

Differentiator
Full-Service Offering

Photo + cinematic video + drone aerial + model/talent — all under one brand. No competitor in Bali offers this complete package.

Innovation
AI-Powered Differentiator

AI booking assistant for 24/7 instant responses and an AI shoot planner that recommends locations, timing, and outfits. Zero competitors have this.

Revenue
Funnel Pricing Strategy

$59 hook captures volume and reviews. $149–249 sweet spot drives main revenue. $399–799 premium packages deliver highest margins. $1,200+ weddings for top-tier revenue.

Growth
SEO-First Acquisition

SEO specialist capability is the unfair advantage. Organic traffic compounds over time while paid ads cost money. Goal: dominate “Bali photographer” search results.

10 — AI-Powered Differentiator

The Innovation
Moat

AI-powered tools that no competitor in Bali offers — creating a technology moat that is difficult to replicate.

10.1 AI Booking Assistant

What It Does

  • 24/7 chatbot on website (WhatsApp integration optional)
  • Answers FAQs: pricing, availability, locations, weather
  • Guides client through package selection based on their needs
  • Handles booking flow: date selection → package → add-ons → payment
  • Sends confirmation + pre-shoot questionnaire automatically
  • Follows up post-shoot for reviews

Why It Matters

  • Tourists browse at all hours (different time zones)
  • Reduces response time from hours to seconds
  • Professional impression — “this studio has their act together”
  • No competitor in Bali has this

Tech Stack

  • Website chatbot (Tidio, Crisp, or custom)
  • Connected to booking calendar (Calendly or custom)
  • AI powered by GPT/Claude API with Rocana-specific knowledge base
  • WhatsApp Business API for direct messaging

10.2 AI Shoot Planner

What It Does

  • Client inputs: date, time preference, group size, style preference, occasion
  • Recommends best locations based on time of day, weather forecast, crowd levels
  • Calculates optimal timing for golden hour at specific locations
  • Suggests outfit colors based on location palette and brand aesthetic
  • Generates a visual “shoot plan” PDF with route plan and weather backup alternatives

Why It Matters

  • Reduces pre-shoot planning time for the team
  • Impresses clients with professionalism
  • Creates a “wow” moment before the shoot even starts
  • Increases confidence in booking (reduces cancellations)
  • No competitor offers anything remotely like this

“Your AI-powered shoot planner will design the perfect Bali photography experience — from golden hour timing to outfit colors that complement the rice terraces.”
