## 1. Executive Summary

**Rocana.Studio** is a Bali-based photography & videography studio targeting international tourists, expats, and destination event clients. The studio offers a full-service creative package (photo + video + drone + model talent) — a combination that no single competitor in Bali currently provides under one brand.

**Core Strategy:** Enter the market with an accessible "hook" price ($59 for a basic session) to build volume, reviews, and SEO authority, then funnel clients into higher-value packages ($250-800+) through upselling. Differentiate through AI-powered booking/planning tools, full-service capability, and a distinctive earthy visual brand.

**Target Revenue (Year 1):** $60,000-96,000 USD
**Target Revenue (Year 3):** $180,000-300,000 USD

## 2. Market Landscape

### 2.1 Bali Photography Market Tiers

| Tier | Price Range (per session) | Players | Volume |
|------|--------------------------|---------|--------|
| **Budget** | $30-100 | Airbnb Experiences, local freelancers | Very High |
| **Mid-range** | $100-350 | Local studios, direct-booking photographers | High |
| **Premium Platform** | $325-715 | Flytographer, Localgrapher | Medium |
| **Premium Studio** | $350-650+ | Gusmank, Bali Pixtura, Apel Photography | Medium-Low |
| **Luxury Wedding** | $650-3,000+ | Established wedding specialists | Low |

### 2.2 Demand by Service Type (ranked)

1. **Pre-wedding / Engagement** — #1 volume driver. Massive demand from Asian couples (China, Korea, Singapore, Taiwan, Hong Kong)
2. **Honeymoon photography** — Strong from Western couples (US, UK, Australia, Europe)
3. **Surprise proposals** — Growing niche, high emotional value = high willingness to pay
4. **Destination weddings** — Lower volume, highest revenue per client
5. **Family / vacation portraits** — Steady, especially families with young children
6. **Solo / travel portraits** — Growing, driven by social media culture
7. **Content creation** — Emerging: influencers, brands wanting Bali-aesthetic content
8. **Corporate / industrial** — Events, retreats, company documentation

### 2.3 Seasonality

| Period | Season | Demand | Strategy |
|--------|--------|--------|----------|
| Apr-Oct | Dry / **PEAK** | Highest (Jul-Aug absolute peak) | Maximize bookings, premium pricing |
| Dec-Jan | Wet but **HOLIDAY SPIKE** | High (Christmas/NYE tourists) | Target holiday travelers |
| Jan-Feb | **CNY MINI-PEAK** | Medium-High | Target Asian pre-wedding market |
| Nov, Feb-Mar | **LOW** | Lowest | Discount packages, content creation, portfolio building, platform review farming |

1. Tegalalang Rice Terraces, Ubud (most booked on Flytographer)

3. Blangsinga Waterfall

### 7.1 Pricing Philosophy: The Funnel

```
$59 HOOK ──→ $149-249 SWEET SPOT ──→ $399-799 PREMIUM ──→ $1,200+ LUXURY
  (volume)      (main revenue)         (high margin)        (weddings)
```

- **$59** = Loss-leader. Covers costs, builds reviews, captures leads.
- **$149-249** = Where most revenue comes from. High volume, good margin.

## 10. AI-Powered Differentiator

### 10.1 AI Booking Assistant

**What it does:**
- 24/7 chatbot on website (WhatsApp integration optional)
- Answers FAQs: pricing, availability, locations, weather
- Guides client through package selection based on their needs
- Handles booking flow: date selection → package → add-ons → payment
- Sends confirmation + pre-shoot questionnaire automatically
- Follows up post-shoot for reviews

**Why it matters:**
- Tourists browse at all hours (different time zones)
- Reduces response time from hours to seconds
- Professional impression — "this studio has their act together"
- No competitor in Bali has this

**Tech stack suggestion:**
- Website chatbot (Tidio, Crisp, or custom)
- Connected to booking calendar (Calendly or custom)
- AI powered by GPT/Claude API with Rocana-specific knowledge base
- WhatsApp Business API for direct messaging

### 10.2 AI Shoot Planner

**What it does:**
- Client inputs: date, time preference, group size, style preference, occasion
- AI recommends:
  - **Best locations** based on time of day, weather forecast, crowd levels
  - **Optimal timing** for golden hour at specific locations
  - **Outfit suggestions** based on location colors and brand aesthetic
  - **Route plan** with travel times between locations
  - **Weather backup** alternative locations if rain is forecasted
- Generates a visual "shoot plan" PDF that client receives before the session

**Why it matters:**
- Reduces pre-shoot planning time for the team
- Impresses clients with professionalism
- Creates a "wow" moment before the shoot even starts
- Increases confidence in booking (reduces cancellations)
